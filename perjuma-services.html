FarmSight: The Complete Farm Management Ecosystem

FarmSight is a sophisticated but easy-to-use platform that combines four powerful technologies to protect and grow your investment. We have simplified high-level science into a tool that delivers clear advice straight to your phone.

Continuous Satellite Monitoring:

Higher Production

We use advanced satellite data to track your crops every five days. This allows us to see how your plants are growing across the entire farm and identify areas that need extra attention.

The Benefit

Boost your total harvest by up to 30% through better crop management.

Smart Soil Sensors

Cost Savings

We place small, durable sensors in the ground to "listen" to your soil. They measure moisture, temperature, and vital nutrients (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium) in real-time so you know exactly when to water or fertilize.

The Benefit

Reduce your spending on water, fertilizer, and pesticides by roughly 25-30% by using them more accurately.

Precision Drone Surveys:

Premium Quality

For a closer look, we use drones equipped with special cameras to map your farm with centimeter-level detail. This helps us verify crop health and create precise "prescription maps" so you only apply chemicals where they are actually needed.

The Benefit

Increase the amount of top-grade, sellable produce you grow by over 60%.

AI Disease Detection:

Reduced Loss

Our artificial intelligence has been trained on thousands of images of local crops to identify diseases with over 95% accuracy. It provides a 7 to 14-day early warning for common threats like fungal infections or viral wilts.

The Benefit

Cut down on crop loss by nearly 50% thanks to early warnings and faster intervention.
