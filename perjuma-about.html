Our Technology

Five Pillars of Precision Agriculture

Satellite Intelligence

We use advanced multispectral satellite data to monitor every hectare of your farm every five days, identifying growth anomalies and stress zones before they escalate.

IoT Soil Sensors

In-ground sensors deliver real-time readings of soil moisture, temperature, and key nutrients so farmers know precisely when and where to irrigate or fertilize.

AI Disease Detection

Our deep-learning models, trained on thousands of local crop images, detect disease and pest threats with 95%+ accuracy — delivering 7–14 days of early warning.

Precision Agriculture

We combine geospatial mapping, variable-rate technology, and field-specific analytics to help farmers apply the right inputs at the right place and time — maximising yield while minimising waste.
