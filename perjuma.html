What We Offer

Smart Solutions for Modern Agriculture

At Perjuma AgTech, we specialize in high-tech agricultural services that take the guesswork out of farming. Our approach combines satellite monitoring, ground sensors, and artificial intelligence to give a complete picture of farm health. Whether it is detecting a plant disease before it spreads or managing water more efficiently, our solutions are designed to help farmers make better decisions and achieve better results.

Research & Development (Tech / AI)

We develop cutting-edge agricultural technologies and AI models to solve real-world farming challenges. Our R&D pipeline spans computer vision for crop disease, predictive yield modelling, and precision input optimisation — building the science that powers smarter agriculture.

SaaS Licensing

Access our proprietary FarmSight platform and AI models through flexible SaaS licensing. We offer tiered plans for agribusinesses, cooperatives, and development organisations — giving you direct access to satellite monitoring, disease detection, and soil analytics without building from scratch.

Agricultural Data Services

End-to-end agric data services — from field-level collection via IoT sensors, drones, and satellite imagery, to advanced processing and predictive analysis. We turn raw agricultural data into actionable intelligence for farmers, institutions, and policy makers.
